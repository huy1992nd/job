About Us

A Right Place to Stay

PRAGUE hotel is an international hotel that will offer guests a comfort and high-quality stay. Visitors can enjoy a breathtaking sea view and panoramic city view while using our swimming pool, gym, and lobby bar. Right on Duong Dinh Nghe Street, which is the most beautiful and convenient location in Da Nang, Prague Hotel is close to the romantic My Khe beach – among the most beautiful beaches on the planet by the US Forbes magazine and gives guests quick access to every famous sightseeing.

Room & Suites

With a luxurious and spacious interior including 55 rooms with full amenities, modern facilities and friendly staff. The hotel not only features deluxe and sophisticated rooms in the interior and exterior layout but also impresses with a lobby bar, gym and a swimming pool on the rooftop with sea view and panoramic view of the city.
